About Us

How it all started…

Thousands of people all over India order food online on a daily basis. However, what one doesn’t realize is that ordering affordable food online is next to impossible. Whether you’re ordering a simple sandwich or a delicious dessert, everything is priced at double the original rate. And if by chance, you do manage to get an affordable food delivery deal online, the packaging the food arrives in is so awful that it will kill your hunger right then and there.

Our first business…

That’s where Yome steps in. A play on the words ‘You’ and ‘Me’, Yome signifies the relation we share with one another. How we landed Yome’s first business is an interesting story. While searching the streets for affordable food one evening, we came across a small stall that sold a variety of items at a reasonable cost while maintaining good hygiene standards. When we asked the owner of the stall as to how he planned to grow his business, he had no answer. That’s when we introduced the concept of Yome to him and he absolutely loved it! And just like that, Yome had its first business!
