Yome

Yome is an online restaurant aggregator startup that gathers information from restaurants all over India on a regular basis.If ordering affordable food online has always been your concern, allow Yome to come to your rescue!

Here’s what Yome aims to accomplish:

  • To partner with restaurants and food outlets all over India and help them grow their business by improving their packaging

  • and overall hospitality. To provide virtual budget-friendly restaurants through online food delivery channels.

We plan to do this, by addressing the following problems:

  • Availability of cost-effective food at your doorstep

  • Assurance of food quality

  • Packaging and handling issues

  • Food outlet owners who don’t have enough knowledge of the latest marketing techniques

The good news is that we aren’t just restricting ourselves to the budget-friendly restaurants; we will also be featuring premium as well as luxury restaurants for customers who would love to splurge on food once in a while!
